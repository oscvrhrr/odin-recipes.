Breakfast

A quick and convinient meal to kickstart your day.

Peanut butter bagel

Ingridients

  • plain bagel
  • peanut butter
  • banana (choice of fruit)
  • bottled protien shake

Macronutrients

  • protein: 35 grams
  • carbs: 65 grams
  • fats: 15 grams

Cooking instructions

  1. slice and toast the bagel
  2. smear 25 grams of peanut butter on bagel
  3. slice 65 grams of banana ( or fruit of choice) and put on bagel
  4. open protien shake
  5. enjoy

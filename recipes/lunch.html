Lunch

Avocado Chicken sandwich

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • sliced bread
  • 1 avocados
  • lettuce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • pineapple
  • mango

Macronutrients

  • protien: 35 grams
  • fats: 20 grams
  • carbs: 105 grams

Cooking instructions

  1. grill 4 oz of chicken breast
  2. toast bread
  3. slice tomato and onion
  4. add avocado to mixing bowl and add salt
  5. blend the avocado with a spoon until get the desired consistency
  6. dice mango and pineapple and serve 250 grams of each
  7. enjoy

Dinner

Chicken Souvlaki

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • tomatoes
  • mint
  • 1 red onion
  • chicken breast
  • greek yougurt
  • pita bread
  • oil, salt, pepper, dried oregano, chili flakes

Macronutrients

  • protien: 42 grams
  • carbs:54 grams
  • fat: 9 grams

Cooking instructions

  1. grate the cucumber and add salt in a bowl
  2. chop chicken breast and add it to a bowl. add a few spoon fulls of yogurt, oregano,pepper and chili flakes, and salt over the chicken.
  3. add lemon juice and grate a clove of garlic onto the chicken.
  4. move the chicken onto a tray and bake it in the oven.
    5. li> dice the onion and tomatoes and mix onto a bowl
  5. now we will prepare the tzatziki. add yogurt onto a bowl and add the grated cucumber from step 1, add half a lemon juice, salt, add mint and stir.
  6. its time to assemble. add tzatziki on pita bread, then add tomatoes and onions
  7. lastly add the chicken on top and sprinkle some mint for garnish
